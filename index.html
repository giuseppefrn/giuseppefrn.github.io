Background

In 2018 I obtained my Bachelor's degree, and in 2020 my Master's degree in computer science at the University of Catania. Also in 2020, I started my PhD, delving into machine learning techniques for anomaly detection initially, and then I worked on colour measurement using images. During my PhD I was able to have very positive experiences, despite the uncertain first year due to COVID-19. I attended conferences, workshops, many interesting seminars and summer schools. And last but not least, I undertook a visiting period as a guest researcher at the Radboud University in Nijmegen (currently ongoing).

About Me

I was born in 1996 and grew up in Gagliano Castelferrato a small town in the Sicilian hinterland, you can admire its beauty in the image above, snow is uncommon but when it happens it is enchanting. After high school I moved to catania to pursue my studies in computer science.

Before my studies, I was passionate about computer science because I found that be able to control a machine (PC) through programming and exploit it to one's liking or need was interesting and exciting. Later, while studying data science and machine learning, I became passionate about these topics. To this day, I still believe it is fantastic to be able to use the data we collect on a daily basis and exploit it to gain benefits in practically all areas. I strongly believe that machine learning will play a fundamental role in the challenges we are going to face as human beings.
